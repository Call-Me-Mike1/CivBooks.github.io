Literature has been known to be the source of a time before, a source of knowledge to all. This is no different in CivClassic, as many, many authours write books daily to show to the server how important literature truly is.
Page 3 of 7
While some write books and other media as a joke, some use the opportunity to write historical non-fiction about an event which occurred in the server's storied past. An example of this non-fiction is with §oColumbia's Fall§0 by SortByNode.
Page 4 of 7
This book outlines the beginnings of SortByNode's journey in CivClassic (Previously CivCraft) through their own eyes, and how they became "CivCraft's Deity" through founding a city and a whole religion/church.
Page 5 of 7
On the subject of religion, literature in CivClassic can be used to create religious/sacred text. Examples include §oBook of Mormon§0 by jeffreyindy and §oThe Testament§0 by Charlieseeese.
Page 6 of 7
Lastly, some use CivClassic's vast literary minds to spark revolution in the reader with political manifestos. These have become so popular in recent years that Icenia's library has an entire section devoted to Communist literature, predominantly in the form of propaganda.
