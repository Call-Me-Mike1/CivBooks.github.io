A Skeleton stopped Ender along his trek across the open plains. He spoke: "come child, a land of sublime beauty and glory awaits you." His sword, doubly sharp, handed to Ender to wield power and authority over all those worthy and just. His bow,
Page 2 of 3
invincible and deadly, handed to Ender so reach the farthest lands. And, lastly, his crown, breezed, blessed, and dazzling, to keep a chilled mind for subtle thought and endurance through troubled times. Ender spoke: "Of why the honour bestowed me, sir?"
Page 3 of 3
The Skeleton replied: "I am the king of a long dead kingdom of gods, my bones holy on this earth yet dust in the next. To you, I pass my talismen, enchanted tools of glory, in the steadfast hope that an empire on this earth may exceed mine in heaven."
