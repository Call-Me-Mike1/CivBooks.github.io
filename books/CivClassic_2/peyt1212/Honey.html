For thousands of years we have known it's flavor, felt the way it seeps past the tongue and down the throat. It has since baffled us in every way, from its strange healing properties to its ability to last thousands of years without a hint of rot, immortal in its golden splendor.

It does not, however, solely rely on the antics of bees as a pathway into our world. Honey can actually be found running through the veins of our own humble breed. One just has to be clever enough to find it.
