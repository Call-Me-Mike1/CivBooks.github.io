For eons, man has sought the perfect companion to spend their lonely days with. Other people are complicated, anti-thesis to the peace we seek in our busy lives. Animals have their spots in the hearts of man, but lack the empathy we crave.

So what is the answer to our conundrum? If we want a middle ground, what choice do we have but to create our own? Catboys are the perfect compromise in a companion, the apex of support and affection. Logical thought can only lead you to this conclusion.

Catboys eat the same foods we do, with an inclination for seafood. They also make for a lifelong companion, with lifespans into their 80s. They wear clothes, and can be trained to bring into public places and most modes of transportation.

Immensely convenient to create and care for, catboys are infact the perfect companion hybrid for the casual consumer. Consider a program for catboys in your local mineman nation today, and you'll see a marked inprovement in moral from the first day.
