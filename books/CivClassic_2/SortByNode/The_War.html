A war was declared, with combatants from both sides meeting up at Fort Blackwell, the Federation's old Capitol. In total, there were eleven members, prepared for war. Diamond enchanted armor, and diamond enchanted swords, as well as our fastest horses.
Page 6 of 24
The time had come to launch a pre-emptive strike against Ares Industries, by pearling daddo. Once we had all gathered, we galloped towards Greyshore, the home of the Ares Mercenary.
Page 7 of 24
We went up the Angstrom canal, which miraculously held the weight of our horses and armor. We finally reached the river that we would follow to Greyshore. This river symbolized that there was no turning back.
Page 8 of 24
