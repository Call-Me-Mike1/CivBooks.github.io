I stayed on my island for a while, doing typical hermit things before I got bored of the isolation and poverty inherent in such a lifestyle.
Page 20 of 47
Near to my island was the city of Mt Augusta - one of the oldest continuously populated cities on the server, along with Aristopolis, Panneton and Haven, and also one of the cities most open to new citizens.
Page 21 of 47
I was given a plot and built a small house, and began to immerse myself in the city and its people. In Mount Augusta was the then most active LSIF commune, Ataraxia.
Page 22 of 47
Intrigued by their ideology (§f.§0Libertarian Socialism or Anarcho-Communism§f.§0) I asked to join, and after a short trial period and the symbolic offering of charcoal produced at the commune, I was given membership.
Page 23 of 47
This was the beginning of the best time I had on 1.0. I spent the vast majority of my 1.0 playtime as a member of the LSIF.
Page 24 of 47
It would take far too long to write out everything I did with them, but the short version is that they were great friends and we built cities, produced surpluses and spread our ideology as far as we could.
Page 25 of 47
