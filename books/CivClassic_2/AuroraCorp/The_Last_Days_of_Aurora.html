§lThe NAU Crisis§0 §0 §0 Naunet was rich in diamonds, but the other shards were not. A diaspora of foreign miners and eager imperialists tore through Naunet, claiming and defacing as they went. Many miners felt entitled to the right to mine
Page 25 of 44
wherever they want, refusing to leave even when they dug under cities and into homes. The Naunetians were highly disturbed by this mindset, and Auroran diplomats proposed a solution.§0 §0 §0 The Naunet Aquatic Union, or NAU, would be an alliance whose
Page 26 of 44
purpose was to prevent further foreign claims from appearing on Naunet. Its second purpose was to contain the destruction caused by miners by forcing them to dig only in allocated areas. At first this sounds like simple and justified isolationism, but in
Page 27 of 44
reality it was an Auroran power play. The Aurorans knew the rest of the server would not react kindly to this, and hoped to utilize their new doctrine of hybrid warfare to make themselves look like the only real innocents in Naunet.§0 §0

It worked. The entire server was in an uproar after a Concordian diplomat leaked out of context pictures from the negotiations making it seem like the NAU would kill anyone mining outside the international zone, when in reality stray miners would

Page 30 of 44
NAU and began its propaganda campaign. The subreddit was flooded with memes, posters, and arguments against Concordia, calling them out for their wall. The campaign was a success, and most of the players in the server were imprinted with the concept that
Page 31 of 44
Concordia was bad. The PR backlash against Concordia was awesome in proportions. The wall was torn down and Concordias prime minister resigned. Concordia also steadily began losing players, until eventually the entire city died. Aurora's foe
