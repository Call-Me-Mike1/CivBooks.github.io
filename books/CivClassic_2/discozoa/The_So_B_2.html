The battle carried on this way for what seemed like hours, and in this way Spongesuds felt he was invincible. Slaying enemies unfortunate enough to decide to go after him, the King faltered not in his relentless slaughter of the enemy.

His entire body racked with pain, the King fell as he quickly began to lose feeling in his body. Another strike, hitting him right in the shoulder, was an arrow. A third attack then came when an axe was driven into his back, and out again.

The King couldn't help it, and was forced to the ground, dying. Taking refuge on the Earth that was bathed in blood, the blood that was leaving his body through his numerous wounds then mingled with the fluids already on the grass,

joined by even more blood that the King was now violently coughing up. He laid on his side as his vision became blurry, not helped by a kick in the face he received by a fleeing soldier who tripped on his body.
