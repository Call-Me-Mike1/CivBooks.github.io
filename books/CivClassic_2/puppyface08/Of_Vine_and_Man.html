Vinekind has, perhaps, existed for as long as Mankind. Legends of old tell of mighty vines and mighty men fighting the forces of evil side by side; doubtlessly the tales have grown taller with time, but they present an undeniable truth regarding the
Page 3 of 10
comradery of the two races.§0 §0 §0When the first explorers of Mankind set foot in foreign jungles, it was Vines who protected them from deadly falls and allowed them to traverse the broken terrain. This friendship has
Page 4 of 10
remained unbroken throughout the aeons. Wherever Man lays roots, Vine has already grown. The Vine does not resist Man's expansion, for he is a friend. Man takes Vine with him and spreads him, and the two races are brothers against the hostile world.

Vine is ubiquitous. In every city, every town, every village, vine grows, helping and healing and spreading. Vinekind lived in ancient Agora; It walked the streets of aytos long after Mankind left for good. Still Vine grows, helping, healing, spreading.

Civilization rises and falls; cities are built, then put to the sword. Mankind, in its own fashion, endures. Vinekind too endures, a living symbol of a bygone age where comradery and friendship were more common.§0 §0 §0 §4* * *

In recent times, Man has begun to forget his old ally. Already there are those who will not tolerate the sight of Vine growing on the wall. They shut their hearts to the healing, to the help, to the spreading. They kill the vine, brutally. They make it
Page 8 of 10
languish in forgotten cells deep underground or burn in a fiery conflageration. The vine forgives. Still it strives to grow, helping and healing and spreading. It gives love to the cold hearts of its enemies. In return, it receives fire and steel.

You cold men, made of stone and ice, I beg you, please: Let the vine grow. Let it help and heal and spread. See the terraces of your home covered in green, and rejoice in the beauty of the day. Open the dungeon cells, and return the friendship that was

given freely throughout the countless years. Take the outstretched hand that never faltered, and step into the dawn of a more peaceful world, a world of Vine and Man.§0 §0 §2~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
