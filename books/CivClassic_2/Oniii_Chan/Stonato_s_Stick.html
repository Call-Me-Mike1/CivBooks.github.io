This certificate gaurentees the authenticity of the stick named "Stonato's 0,0 Factory Stick".
Page 2 of 5
It was found by Amelorate / Oniii_Chan in an ancient factory room about 30 blocks away from Stonato's 0,0 bunker. Inside this factory room there was a factory named RAIL FACTORY. Similarly inside another bunker at 0,0 there was another rail factory written and stylized like so.
Page 3 of 5
The obsidian this bunker was built with was decayed to the exact same amount as other blocks known to be placed by stonato. Thus, it is assumed that the bunker at 0,0, the 0,0 bunker, and the factory room were all owned by stonato.

Thusly, the stick, which was hanging in an item frame, was highly likely to be owned by Stonato.
