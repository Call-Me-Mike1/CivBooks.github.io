Anime and Arson May 10th, 2021 This morning, I woke up and logged into CivClassics. After running around in circles for a few minutes, as I always start my day, I came across ICENIA MAPS.

anime bad

I immediately checked snitches, and found the arsonist was the newfriend who had joined Icenia just days prior: squ8i. I immediately told the Icenian Militia he was POS, however Charlieseeese told me he was nice, and shouldn't be pearled.
Page 6 of 16
As a result, I opened a case in the justice channel of the Icenia discord. I provided evidence of the crimes committed, and argued that the crimes committed were in fact a hate crime against anime lovers in Icenia. As a result, I requested fair reperations and began the case.
Page 7 of 16
The newly elected Grand Judge 1drop took the case, but failed to tell the defendant that he had a right to a lawyer. Being the nice, peaceful anime lover I was, I told the newfriend this fact.
Page 8 of 16
However, in globalchat, people claimed that this was merely an attempt by big anime to silence the small newfriends of Icenia. This of course is impossible. Why would I have told the newfriend he has a right to a lawyer if I didn't want a fair trial to begin?
Page 9 of 16
As a result of this accusation and others, I ran a poll on the Civclassics server to see how many people love and hate anime.
Page 10 of 16
The results were shocking.
Page 11 of 16
Nearly half of the players on civclassics absolutely despised anime, and would do anything to get rid of it.
Page 12 of 16
This correlated with my own observations. Just the previous month, a bill was proposed in the Icenian Grand Council. It wanted to ban all anime map art within Icenia and banish anime lovers from its borders.
Page 13 of 16
The good judge drop soon came to a judgement. He ordered squ8i to pay 5 diamonds in reperations and undo all damages.
Page 14 of 16
However, this wasn't the end to squ8i's anime-hating crime spree.
Page 15 of 16
The next day, he went to Gensokyo's Sea of Tranquility and attempted to grief the great map art found out at sea.

This lead the full wrath of the world police to come down upon him. Before he managed to do any significant damage, or even for most gensokyoians to awaken, he was soon pearled and brought to true justice for his anime-hating ways.
