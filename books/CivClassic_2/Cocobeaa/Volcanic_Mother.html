Pele'makuawahine is a fire goddess of the Indojic religion. It is believed that Pele sent her grandson Keoki to Earth 3,000 years ago to be the first ruler of Keoko, beginning the divine family of Adinan Emperors.
Page 3 of 14
Pele was given rule over the land by Izanhi when he handed to her his holy medallion.§0 §0Later, in a competition with her sister lono, Pele gave birth to three goddesses, who with lono offspring, are collectively the ancestors of Emperor Keoko.
Page 4 of 14
During this competition, pele was unwilling to admit defeat. This caused lono to furiously wreak havoc throughout the heavens and the Earth. Pele fled into a cave, and her absence caused darkness throughout Adina.

Pele's absence caused much dismay on Earth. As the crops died off and the people suffered, the gods decided they needed to return Pele to her position of power. They sought the help of several dieties,

The creative force in Pele's nature is shown in the synchronization of the elements during the eruption process. All four elements work together. Earth and fire mix creating lava. Lava possesses water characteristics in the liquidity of its form,
