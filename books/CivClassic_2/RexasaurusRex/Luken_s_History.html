An Account of the History of the Lineage of Luken of house Olivan, Emperor of all Rezia, for the glory of the realm and his families. Date: 10.11.6.211. Complied by Gregorius Ancilarius Maximus ===================
Page 2 of 9
The first account must be given to the first family of the land, the family of his Imperial Majesty, Luken I Olivan. The Olivan family hails from the Urricagan province in Haygen. The northern isles from Lyrcia in the land of Rezia.
Page 3 of 9
Their noble lineage dates back to the time that some of the first Haygen migrants to that land. It is rumored that they came from Ollandas, but that has yet to be established. In any case, the first patriarch of the Olivan household was Liuvius, who dates back to the e6.
Page 4 of 9
The family has held minor titles throughout that time in Haygen, before acquiring major lands through marriage in the e7 & e8. The family gained in ascendency at Haygen during the time of Richard the Golden, King of the Lyrcians.
Page 5 of 9
They gained great power in Hyagen during the gold age for their patronage of the great trades. Arnaige, the capital city of Haygen, became a major trade center under their leadership, and they became among the most wealthy independent families.
Page 6 of 9
It was the Haygens who, during the first great war, sent many legions in the conquest. Their contribution to the war effort was critical in the north. Their legions were landed in Corvun, and marched to the relief of the capital.
Page 7 of 9
It was Orlan I who commanded these legions. At the conclusion of these battles, and at the end of the war, the Tellurans demanded the removal of the Virdan dynasty from the throne. Theobald II stepped aside in order to prevent further bloodshed and effusion.
Page 8 of 9
It was then that Orlan the first, the great-grandfather of the Emperor our noble lord, was elected as Emperor of all Rezia and established the royal house of Olivan that has succeeded now four generations.
