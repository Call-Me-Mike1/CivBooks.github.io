We as vinlander residents and citizens should call for a collection of written laws. These laws should be written not just on subreddits and Google documents but also and mainly in books. These books should be stored in chests.
Page 3 of 4
All residents and citizens should be allowed to access these books. Residents and citizens can then write their own laws, which would then pass given that enough people supported the new law. I belive this will lead to greater transparancy in vinland.

It will also serve as an interesting experiment. Not only having meaningful impact on the city but also making the game more fun for those interested in this area of the game. Let the people keep the power.
