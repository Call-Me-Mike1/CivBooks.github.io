§lOPINION EDITORIAL§0 §0§l================§0Tides of change with New Election by Wingzero54§0 §0 §0The April election comes on the heels of some election reform -- That being new rules stating all citizens running for council must publicly
Page 14 of 24
announce on the subreddit, and vote tallies will not be released until the end of voting. The rules were enacted to disallow stealth candidacies, such as BG who did not publicly announce running, but turned up on the roster on voting week.
Page 15 of 24
They also were passed in the hope of reducing the amount of voting which was done based on seeing the vote count, which would push for voting for who you want, not who you want to keep in or out depending on the current tally.§0 §0This may be the first
Page 16 of 24
election on CivClassics in which not many old players ran - only Bgbba and MightyOddish ran from the old group, and myself being a junior councillor. The vast majority of candidates were instead all newcomers to Yoahtl, most having been a part of the
Page 17 of 24
Yoahtl community for under a year. This is a sign of a changing of the players and mindset of Yoahtl - a topic which I wrote about in my previous op ed during the last election. Yoahtl is no longer filled with original players who are immersed in the lore

§lGLOBAL NEWS§0 §0===================1.12 Update Brings Diamond Crisis!§0 §0NEW YOAHTL CITY: While the new update to 1.12 has brought the players of CivClassics many new features to enjoy, such as ExilePearl, city bastions, concrete and terracotta, it hasn't
Page 21 of 24
arrived without some issues. The switch to the newest release of the game has affected the HiddenOre plugin's ore spawn rates, and subsequently made diamonds extremely rare and difficult to find. For more established players, this has little impact, as
Page 22 of 24
they still primarily acquire diamonds through commerce and trade, however it hampers a newfriend's ability to make create wealth and establish themselves in the server. Although the mods are working hard fixing this issue and responding to
Page 23 of 24
the slurry of modmail that have been sent in response to this bug, many of the newfriends who joined after the update still have next to no diamonds, further hurting the already low newfriend retention rates. The increasing scarcity of diamonds is also
Page 24 of 24
affecting the value of other mediums of currency, particularly iron. Hopefully the mods will continue working to fix this bug and stabilize the economy and allow newfriends to progress more easily.
