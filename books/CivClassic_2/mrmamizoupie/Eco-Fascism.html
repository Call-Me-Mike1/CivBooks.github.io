Throughout Civ-Servers, there has been a consistent effort to preserve the natural state of the world as it is. However, most of these efforts are in vain due to the fact that newfriends destroy the natural beauty in order to satisfy their short sighted

needs. In this civclassic iteration, there is a group of people willing to hunt down and pearl people that are ruining the natural state and ecosystem of the naturally generated world. This is what they call, Eco-Fascists.

What is an Eco-Fascist?§0 §0 §0An Eco-Fascist is an individual that practices Eco-preservation, but taken up to a higher degree. They are violent and psychopathic, ready to kill anyone who dares ruin the natural beauty of the world.
Page 5 of 12
For example: if someone decides to create a sky farm, they will not only tear down the farm, but also wait out until the owner comes, and will pearl them on sight. §0 §0 §0It may seem that they are quite an awful type of folk, but that is not quite a case.
