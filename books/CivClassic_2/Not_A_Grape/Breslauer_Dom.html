The Breslauer Dom, also known as the Breslau Cathedral was built during the reign of King Friedrich I, who died shortly after its completion. Measuring in at 190 blocks long, 90 blocks wide, and 100 blocks tall; the Breslau Cathedral is one of
Page 3 of 8
the largest and most imposing stuctures in Civcraft. The lead architect for the project was Kaelthas88 of Prussia, whose winning design was selected by popular aclaim over competing designs.

Widely considered to be one of the great wonders of Civcraft 2.0 and a testament to the Prussian legacy, the Breslau Dom is an intergral part of the Prussian character. The concept of the Cathedral was already being discussed within the first week of 2.0

and its construction was only a matter of time and resources- Ordung demanded that the Prussian people build a great and wonderous cathedral for the world to see and Ordung's is an irresistable force of the Prussian character.
