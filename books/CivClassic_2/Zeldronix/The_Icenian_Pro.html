Chapter 1 "A Threat" As we all know icenia is a power that borders us on all sides by sea or river and in some instances land. It is by land their existence as a threat to truidencian soverignty is best seen. And I assure you, that threat is very real.
Page 3 of 18
On the doorstep of our capitol, the Rubicon-Dol Morhen border, there has been sightings of work being done for the demolition of Old Carbon by Icenia. When inquired about this I was responded with that there was a wish to expand their farms in that area.
Page 4 of 18
Naturally I did not believe these lies, for who in their right mind would build farms on a border between nations? No, there has to be another reason. It is my belief that they are building something there they they wish to keep secret.

A wild claim I know. But look at the facts. The icenians have proven themselves to be truely barbarous with no respect for the truidencian people or even the sovereignty of our great nation time and time again. They even kill our own in our very capitol.
