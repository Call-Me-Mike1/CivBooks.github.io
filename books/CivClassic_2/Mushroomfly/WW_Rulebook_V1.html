Wool Wars Guide 1. Wool Wars is played between two Teams, each with 12 players. Teams will elect their Team Captain and Lieutenant, who will be Player 1 and 7, respectively. 2. Both teams will be given sets of armor. The Team Leader and Lieutenant will wear caps of a-

-Crossbows, and ammunition are allowed to be taken by the Team Captain. 7. The match time is twenty (20) minutes. If needed, play can be timed-out for five (5) minutes if a Team Captain informs a Referee a Player requires a pause. 8. The Play Area is divided into a 9x9-

-grid of square Zones, with each Zone itself being made of 9x9 Blocks. The goal of Wool Wars is to control a majority of the Zones by covering more Blocks in the Zones with a Team's colored Wool than the other Team. 9. A location with Wool on it already can be contested by-
