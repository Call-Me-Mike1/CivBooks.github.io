As you may already know St. Firox is the patron saint of travellers and hermits. He is highly regarded in Firoxus Swamp. So much in fact, that people have dedicated much time in researching him and other saints. This area of study know to few.

In Firoxus, it is known as Firoxa. The important thing to remember is that Firoxa is not only the term for the study but also the term for those who study it.

There are many schools of thought when discussing the Firoxa, however in regards to St. Firox there are mainly three. those three are. Heliopyr, Gidruius, and the Necrult.
