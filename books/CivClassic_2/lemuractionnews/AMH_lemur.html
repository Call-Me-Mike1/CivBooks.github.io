AMH lemur Exhibit Lemur was a roaming adventurer on civcraft 2.0. He was affiliated with Aytos and Bohemia, but he didn't really stick around those places for long.
Page 2 of 8
The legend goes that Lemur, and his band of adventurers, sought out ancient knowledge. In those days, they were known as the LemLay company, and they guarded the libraries of bohemia.
Page 3 of 8
On the hunt for more knowledge and adventure, the LemLay company ventured into the endless marshes on devoted. There, they became the people known as the grots. They set up their homeland as Grotmarsh.
Page 4 of 8
The grots, lead by lemur, found the columbians on the short lived server, expand. He followed them to Civclassics, slowly becoming a core member. He was granted Apoland and the grots became the Apolish.
Page 5 of 8
You can read a much more detailed version of this history in the book "oral history of the grots". Lemur served something like 7 terms as leader of Columbia. These days, he aims to retire and focus on building.
Page 6 of 8
Though he is not some great warrior, he is an accomplished statesman and scholar. He is a maester. He also really hates macaroni salad. His vision for Apoland is a city free from building restriction

and safe for all builders and roleplayers.
