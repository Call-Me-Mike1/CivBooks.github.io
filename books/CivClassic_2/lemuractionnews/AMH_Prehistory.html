AMH Prehistory Exhibit The era of Apolish history from before the arrival of Lemur and the grots has few sources. Much of what we know is from Shadedoom and KnotNSA as well as scattered archeological clues.
Page 2 of 25
Nevertheless, what follows is a telling of Apolish history from the earliest known details to the establishment of the second empire of Columbia.

The Island of Apoland was generated as a plains biome. This is somewhat rare for this part of the map. Most of Hallow and mainland Columbia is extreme hills and forest. Where Apoland Proper sits now, originally was
Page 4 of 25
a Villager village. According to old maps, the rest of the island was mostly devoid of trees, but this could have been part of an early attempt at colonization by the new swedish or another group.
Page 5 of 25
some believe that a few of the buildings in downtown Apoland are actually remnants of the village that have been modified, but there is little evidence of this.
Page 6 of 25
The island laid essentially untouched until the summer of 2017, when the Grand Duchy of Columbia settled Rotterdam, and claimed the island. Apoland was claimed by Furst Shadedoom to be his personal estate. He named it Cascadia.
Page 7 of 25
Despite this, Shade did not develop the island. He soon lapsed into a state of inactivity and the island languished. A month or two passed and it was soon forgotten that Shade had claimed the island. Then Shade returned.
Page 8 of 25
He found his land had been settled on and granted to another fursten, Aposii and Green_trump. They had named their colony Apolland. An argument ensued. The settlers didn't know that they were living on claimed land.
Page 9 of 25
By this time, they already had started building. The other noblemen of Columbia reasoned it would be unfair to kick them out. But Shade had been a founding member of the country. He only dropped his claim to the island when he
Page 10 of 25
was offered another island in the south of the country. He called this one Letzebuerg. Now, Apo and Green continued creating their colony. They bulldozed most of the village. They built a dock and several small shacks.
Page 11 of 25
They built the windmill and, most importantly, they tilled a large field on the south of the island. This was important because Apoland was expected to pay taxes to the capital, which took the form of food production.
Page 12 of 25
They mostly grew carrots and potatoes. Then, things get a little hard to track down. All we know is that Apo and green came into a large sum of wealth. One may think they simply must have mined it. After all, in this time,
Page 13 of 25
Apo and green did mine a lot to create their rail network. Yet, their mining strategy (as observed in present day) is completely inadequate to yield any large sum of diamonds or any other ore for that matter.

All we know is that they had riches... and they did not want to share a single diamond. Evidently, the other noblemen found out about their newfound wealth. They demanded that they pay a portion to help the rest of the duchy.
