Process of Brewing§0 §0 §0Brewing can be a very simple task, or can be a very pain stakingly long task. Brewing first starts with a cauldron with fire underneath it, this is used to ferment drinks from 2 min-30 min, you can check with R click with clock,

some drinks only take this stage of brewing (example being Potato Soup, recipe in back).§0 §0 §0Next step can go three ways from here. Distilling, Barreling, or Distilling then barreling. §0 §0 §0Distilling§0 §0Distilling takes a brewing stand that
